Content

Home page

Welcome to Xbox Xpert. We’re brining you the hottest news and most exciting reviews. We’re the new guy in town when it comings to gaming sites, but visit our news page for all the latest headlines, and our “Day One Promise” to you!

Images for the Home page

News

This page will have the newest and most exciting Xbox news headlines, covering upcoming games, as well as industry developments. At Xbox Xpert we want to share the news and discuss our thoughts, so feel free to comment in any of the articles. You can also find any updates regarding the site here as well! In the realm of news content feel free to read up on the most exciting acquisition in recent memory, the 69 billion dollar purchase of Activision Blizzard by Microsoft.

Images for News

Reviews

This page will have all of our newest reviews for all of the hottest games on Xbox. We will be reviewing everything from the biggest AAA games, and the hiddenest of indie gems. Our reviews are from the heart, and to the point. We don’t shill for anyone, and we pay for our games, just like you do (or, Gamepass, because, duh). For now, check out our review of one of the biggest games of the year, and the first new Bethesda IP in over 20 years, Starfield.
